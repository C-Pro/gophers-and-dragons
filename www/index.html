Gophers & Dragons

Choose your avatar
Game overview
This is a simple rogue-like game for Go programmers.
Your avatar is played by your program.
Every turn that program needs to decide which action to perform.
The set of possible actions is defined by your cards deck.
Every card performs some specific action which can be offensive, defensive or tactical.
You can read a full manual to learn all game mechanics.
